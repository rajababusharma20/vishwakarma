About Us

Vishwakarma Traders was established in 1985 by the late Raghuvansh Thakur, who belonged to the village of Dighi. In the early days, the late Thakur ji used to sell tawa, kadhai, buckets, sickles and small spades from market to market.

In 1987, due to his sudden death, his son Suresh Thakur took over the shop's business in 1988. After that, he started trading in sand, cement, and iron. Gradually, he expanded into paints, hand pumps, pipes, boring, submersibles, sheet metal, and plumber fittings.

Afterwards, in 2020, his grandson Raja Thakur took over the business. He further expanded it by including Berger Paints, Asian Paints, Ashirvad Pipes & Fittings, Birla Opus Paints, plywood, and many other items.

Over three generations, we have earned the trust and love of our customers, and we will continue to strive to keep this relationship strong forever.
